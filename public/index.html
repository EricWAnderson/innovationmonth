Welcome New Students
to our Community of Makers

In September 2016, we are celebrating interdisciplinary, hands-on, and experiential learning at the University of Minnesota.

Student groups and faculty will come together, hosting events where the class of 2020 will experience our growing interdisciplinary culture.

Foster an Inderdisciplinary Culture

Our University has a wide array of spaces and resources that support hands-on and interdisciplinary learning, but students don't feel welcome to find and join them.

Student Innovation Month changes this. In the first month of Fall 2016, you will help students discover the innovative power of diverse teams.

How You Can Help

Join our email list. Attend our kickoff planning event. Sponsor an event that connects students across disciplines.
